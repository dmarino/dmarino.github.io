About Me

I am a Software Engineer from Bogota, Colombia interested in Front-End Development, Game Programming, and Interactive Design. I like creating fun experiences for all kinds of proposes and pushing the limits of what technology and art are capable of.

Since I started my bachelor’s degree in Los Andes University back in 2014 and during my one-year program in Vancouver Film School, I’ve developed multiple games in all kinds of languages and platforms; I’ve worked with different enterprises developing web applications for their internal use; and I’ve helped hundreds of students as a TA and a research assistant in programming related classes. My goal is to work towards making videogames and technology more accessible and more friendly for all kinds of users through the use of Human Center Design and learn new things along the way.
